Welcome to the Andhra Loyola PG Library

The library provides a peaceful environment for study, rich collections of books, journals, and digital resources to enhance learning and research.

About the Library

The Andhra Loyola College (Autonomous), Vijayawada, maintains a well-equipped Postgraduate Library with extensive resources supporting academic and research needs. It aims to foster knowledge through access to print and digital information. The Andhra Loyola PG Library covers a total carpet area of 1212 square feet and houses a rich collection of 16,629 books across various disciplines. The library maintains 7,998 titles, supporting the academic and research needs of students and faculty. It subscribes to 67 journals, including 40 Indian and 27 foreign publications, providing access to current developments in diverse fields. The library also holds 10 newspapers and magazines, and a multimedia collection of 717 CDs and DVDs. To support digital access, the library offers internet connectivity with a bandwidth of 20 Mbps, along with 35 computer terminals available for users. The library operations are efficiently managed by a team of 2 dedicated staff members.

Library Rules

  • Maintain silence inside the library.
  • Use of mobile phones is strictly prohibited.
  • Books must be returned within the due date.
  • Handle library materials with care.
  • Personal belongings are not allowed inside the reading area.
