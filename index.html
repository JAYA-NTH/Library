Welcome to the Andhra Loyola PG Library

The library provides a peaceful environment for study, rich collections of books, journals, and digital resources to enhance learning and research.

About the Library

The Andhra Loyola College (Autonomous), Vijayawada, maintains a well-equipped Postgraduate Library with extensive resources supporting academic and research needs. It aims to foster knowledge through access to print and digital information. The library covers 1212 sq.ft and houses 16,629 books with 7,998 titles. It subscribes to 67 journals, holds 10 newspapers/magazines, 717 CDs/DVDs, and offers 35 computer terminals with 20 Mbps internet. Managed by 2 dedicated staff members.

Library Rules

  • Maintain silence inside the library.
  • Use of mobile phones is strictly prohibited.
  • Books must be returned within the due date.
  • Handle library materials with care.
  • Personal belongings are not allowed inside the reading area.
